Annotation information:                                                            
  The recordings contain annotations in the script which include disfluencies   
  such as audible breathing, hesitations, external audio and abrupt stops. The  
  additional annotations are denoted by square brackets []. In total, the          
  scripts contain 1306 annotations, 682 for the female recordings and 625 for   
  the male recordings.                                                             
                                                                                   
Annotations:                                                                       
  [abrupt]:                                                                        
  The audio stops abruptly at the end of the recording, possibly cutting off the 
  end of the audio.                                                                
                                                                                   
  [breath]:                                                                        
  Audible breathing or a sniff was heard in the recording.                         
                                                                                   
  [external]                                                                       
  External audio can be heard in the recordings.                                   
                                                                                   
  [hesitation]                                                                     
  Hesitation in the reading of the text.                                           
                                                                                   
  [snap]                                                                           
  A minor snap of the tongue, lip movement  preceding, during (or sometimes        
  following) a line recording.                                                     
                                                                                   
The annotations can be at the start, end or anywhere in the line. Some lines       
contain more than one annotation.                                                  
                                                                                   
Examples:                                                                          
  Ohùn wa ti há. [external]                                                        
  [breath] Ṣé o fẹ́ràn aṣọ tí ẹ̀gbọ́n rẹ fi ránṣẹ́ láti òkè òkun? [external]           
                                                                                   
  In the first example, external sounds were heard at the end of the recording. 
  In the second example breathing was heard at the start of the recording, as   
  well as an external sounds being noticed at the end of the recordings.
